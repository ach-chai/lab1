Playable Characters

Characters are obtainable units in Genshin Impact. They come in different qualities, are aligned with an Element, equippable with a specific Weapon, and belong to a Region.

As of 6.2, there are 109 playable characters. This is a sample page, showcasing only 10 characters per rarity.
